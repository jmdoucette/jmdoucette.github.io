Spotify Unlocked (code)

Spotify Unlocked is a ruby on rails web application that interacts with the spotify api. It can:

  • Get data on songs, albums and artists that are not available on the spotify app
  • Get filtered user data, like favorite songs with few listers or high energy
  • Construct the chords for the song from the audio data
